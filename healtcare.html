healthcare Security

Central Protection Services provides specialized healthcare security for hospitals, community healthcare facilities, medical clinics, and long-term care establishments. Chosen according to their ability to physically and mentally adapt to challenging and rapidly-changing environments, they are thoroughly trained to protect not only the clinical facilities but also other facilities for instance, staff, patients, and visitors, and provide Alarm Guard Security. They also keep prescription medicines secured and medical information confidential.
